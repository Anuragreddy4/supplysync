# SupplySync AI - Live Demo Playbook & Script
**Presentation Duration**: ~5 Minutes
**Target Audience**: Judges, Potential Partners, Tech Leads

---

## Demo Setup (Pre-run Checklist)
1. **Tabs Layout**:
   - Tab 1: [Buyer Dashboard](http://localhost:3000/buyer/dashboard) (Log in as `buyer-demo@supplysync.ai`)
   - Tab 2: [Supplier Dashboard](http://localhost:3000/supplier/dashboard) (Log in as `supplier-demo@supplysync.ai`, split-screen style)
   - Tab 3: Terminal window showing active logs of `services/api` and `services/ai-agents`.
2. **Database Status**: Seeded with default listings and at least 5 nearby buyers in the Kukatpally, Hyderabad area.

---

## Part 1: Buyer Dashboard & Demand Forecasting Agent (0:00 - 1:00)

**Speaker Action**: Open the Buyer Dashboard. Point to the flashing glow effect on the top banner containing the "Demand Forecast Alert".

**Visual On-Screen**:
- A prominent alert: *"Gemini Forecast: Grain inventory estimated to deplete in 5 days."*
- Click **"Expand Reasoning"**. A glassmorphic popover opens, showing a line chart showing past depletion rates and a summary block.

**Script / Speaker Notes**:
> "Good afternoon. We begin our journey on the dashboard of a local retail merchant in Madhapur, Hyderabad. Instead of manually auditing inventory or relying on guesswork, our merchant is greeted by a predictive alert. 
>
> By clicking 'Expand Reasoning', we see the output of our **Demand Forecast Agent**. It has analyzed past sales velocity and local market trends, warning us that our rice inventory will run dry in exactly 5 days with a 92% confidence score. The AI already knows what I will need before I do, taking the administrative burden off the small business owner."

---

## Part 2: Hyperlocal Group-Buys & Pooling (1:00 - 2:00)

**Speaker Action**: Click on the **"Group-Buys"** tab on the navigation bar. Tap to select the *Premium Basmati Rice Pool* which shows "4/5 Members Joined - 80kg to target". Click **"Join Pool"**.

**Visual On-Screen**:
- The group-buy card animates with a pulse glow.
- The progress bar dynamically animates from 80% to 100% (or increases by the buyer's requested quantity).
- A banner appears: *"Target Met! Wholesale price of ₹40/kg unlocked (Saved 15%)."*

**Script / Speaker Notes**:
> "But a small shop owner cannot buy massive truckloads of rice alone to get a discount. That is where our **Group-Buy Matchmaking Agent** steps in.
>
> Under the Group-Buys tab, we see active pools created by neighboring merchants within a 2-kilometer radius. I will join this Basmati Rice pool with a 20kg order. Watch the progress bar animate in real-time as my quantity is added. Instead of buying alone at a bad price, I am pooling with 4 other shop owners nearby, unlocking a bulk discount automatically."

---

## Part 3: Supplier Incoming Queue & Trust Score (2:00 - 3:00)

**Speaker Action**: Switch to the Supplier Dashboard (split screen or separate tab). Point to the incoming order queue. Select the incoming grouped order.

**Visual On-Screen**:
- An incoming order alert flashes.
- The order detail pane shows the buyer list, geocoded addresses, and a **"Supplier Trust Score: 94.50"** badge.
- Hover over the Trust Score. A tooltip displays: *"Based on 98% On-Time Deliveries and +0.5 price consistency adjustment."*

**Script / Speaker Notes**:
> "Now let us swap roles. On the right side of the screen, we see the dashboard of our regional supplier. The order has landed in their queue in near-real-time.
>
> Notice the **Trust Score gauge** in the header. Our platform polices and incentives good logistics practices. This score is generated by our **Trust & Verification Agent**, which analyzes historical delivery timestamps and reviews. Every delivery either builds or costs them trust, visibly. Buyers know they are purchasing from a dependable supplier, eliminating opaque transaction friction."

---

## Part 4: Supplier Listing & Dynamic Fair-Pricing (3:00 - 4:00)

**Speaker Action**: Click **"Create Listing"**. In the price input field, type `₹95.00/kg` (an excessively high price compared to the seed price of ₹45). Point to the warning badge.

**Visual On-Screen**:
- The form input shows a live verification badge.
- As the price goes up, the badge changes from green (`Fair Price Verified`) to a pulsing amber warning: `[WARNING] Price exceeds local average by 110%`.
- An info tip explains: *"Gemini Dynamic Pricing suggests a fair corridor of ₹42.00 - ₹48.00 based on local competitors."*

**Script / Speaker Notes**:
> "To prevent opportunistic gouging, we implement a **Dynamic Pricing Agent**. As the supplier inputs a price for a new listing, the AI verifies local market averages. 
>
> If the supplier inputs ₹95, the system immediately highlights a pulsing amber warning badge. The platform actively polices fair pricing without either side needing to negotiate blind, ensuring the ecosystem remains competitive and healthy."

---

## Part 5: Close on Hyperlocal Density Maps (4:00 - 5:00)

**Speaker Action**: Navigate back to the main map dashboard view. Drag the picker pin slightly to show the instant distance recalculation in the info card.

**Visual On-Screen**:
- A beautifully styled dark-theme Google Map showing markers of buyers and suppliers in Kukatpally and Madhapur, Hyderabad.
- The Distance Indicator card updates instantly showing: *"Distance: 4.8 km"* down to *"Distance: 2.1 km"*.

**Script / Speaker Notes**:
> "Finally, let's look at the mapping view. Here, we see the hyperlocal density of our pilot neighborhood in Hyderabad. The markers represent connected merchants. 
>
> As a supplier or merchant moves their location pin, our client-side Haversine engine performs instant distance calculations, offering optimistic sorting prior to server confirmation.
>
> SupplySync AI unites maps, predictive logistics, and automated agents into a single high-performance platform. It reduces costs, eliminates food wastage, and builds lasting trust. Thank you, and we welcome your questions."
